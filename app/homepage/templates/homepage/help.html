Login

A unique ID is made for each name & password combination. We will store your list of characters under your unique ID. Be sure to write down your username and password so that you can access your list of characters at any time.

Username

We are keeping accounts separate based on a combination of unique username and password. So there can be 100 "John Smith" accounts as long as each one has a different password.

Password

We are not responsible for forgotten passwords. Be sure to keep your password safe and accessible.

Guest account

If you would like us to save a list of characters for you, create an account. Otherwise, you may proceed as a guest. You may create an account any time before you end your session.

Game Help

Check out the links to the Wizards of the Coast website, the official Dungeons and Dragons Player's Handbook, or your Dungeon Master for help in playing the game.
