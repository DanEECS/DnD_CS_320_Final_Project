Help


  • Top Navigation Bar: hover over icon and click on link to navigate to page.

  • Login: a unique ID is made for each name & password combination. We will store your list of characters under your unique ID. Be sure to write down your username and password so that you can access your list of characters at any time.

  • Username: we are keeping accounts separate based on a combination of unique username and password. So there can be 100 "John Smith" accounts as long as each one has a different password.

  • Password: we are not responsible for forgotten passwords.

  • Guest account: you do not need to have a login account to create a character, but you do need a login account in order to have us save a list of characters for you. If you don't want to have a login account, you may proceed as a guest. If you change your mind before you end your session, you may create a login account.

  • Game Help: Please check out the links to the Wizards of the Coast website, the official Dungeons and Dragons Player's Handbook, or your Dungeon Master for help in playing the game.
