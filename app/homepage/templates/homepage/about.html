About


This website was created as a final project CS 320 Software Engineering, a class at Washington State University, Vancouver in the Fall of 2019 under the direction of Dr. Xinghui Zhao. The members of the group were: Daniel Garret, Matthew Fritz, Ruth Woodcock, and Trevor McLam.

  • Artwork comes from the Wizards of the Coast Dungeons and Dragons Fan Site kit. Please see credits and copyright acknowledgement below.

  • Fonts are Google fonts: Oswald, Muli.

  • The database management comes from Django.

  • Semantic UI was used for buttons, forms, and other design elements.
